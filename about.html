What We Do

IceTimeHQ aggregates open ice sessions from rinks across the US and puts them in one place. Public skating, stick & puck, pickup hockey, freestyle — we pull live schedules directly from each rink's booking system so what you see is always current.

No more hunting through rink websites, calling ahead, or showing up to a closed sheet. Just pick your day, pick your session type, and go skate.

Where we're going

We're building the most complete ice rink directory in the US. We're expanding to new regions continuously — and beyond schedules, we're working toward connecting skaters with pro shops, league information, and eventually live rink cameras.

Everything you need to get on the ice, all in one place.
